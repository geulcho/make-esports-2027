# WE, World Event Format PRD

**Document version:** v1.0  
**Event:** WE, World Event  
**Window:** Week 45 to Week 48  
**Participants:** 24 national teams  
**Purpose:** Final national-team championship that crowns the strongest national representative team.

This document defines the WE main-event format only. The prior Intermatch and Interregional Qualifier documents determine the 24 qualified national teams.

---

## 1. Qualification Input

WE contains **24 national teams**.

| Entry path | Teams |
|---|---:|
| Europe direct qualifiers | 8 |
| Asia-Pacific direct qualifiers | 6 |
| Americas direct qualifiers | 4 |
| Middle East & Africa direct qualifier | 1 |
| Interregional Qualifier wildcard teams | 5 |
| **Total** | **24** |

The five IQ winners enter WE as **wildcards**.

---

## 2. Seeding

The 24 qualified teams are seeded by **Week 33 national-team rating** into four pots of six teams.

| Pot | Size | Rule |
|---|---:|---|
| Pot 1 | 6 | Highest-rated eligible teams by Week 33 rating |
| Pot 2 | 6 | Next six eligible teams by Week 33 rating |
| Pot 3 | 6 | Next six eligible teams by Week 33 rating |
| Pot 4 | 6 | All five IQ wildcard teams plus one remaining direct qualifier |

### Pot 4 override

The five IQ wildcard teams are always placed in **Pot 4**, regardless of Week 33 rating. The final Pot 4 slot is filled by the lowest-rated direct qualifier after Pots 1 to 3 are filled.

---

## 3. Group Draw

WE uses **six groups of four teams**: Group A to Group F.

| Item | Rule |
|---|---|
| Groups | 6 groups, A to F |
| Teams per group | 4 |
| Pot composition | Each group receives one team from each pot |
| Region separation | Direct qualifiers from the same region cannot be drawn into the same group |
| Wildcard exception | IQ wildcard teams are region-neutral for draw purposes |
| Europe exception | Because Europe has 8 direct qualifiers, up to two groups may contain two direct European teams; no group may contain three direct European teams |

### Wildcard region neutrality

IQ wildcard teams do **not** count as their original region for group-draw restrictions. For example, a direct-qualified Korea and an IQ-qualified Japan can be drawn into the same group.

---

## 4. Group Stage

The group stage is a **Bo3 single round robin**.

| Item | Value |
|---|---:|
| Groups | 6 |
| Teams per group | 4 |
| Matches per group | 6 |
| Total group-stage matches | 36 |
| Match format | Bo3 |
| Advancement | Group top 2 plus the best four 3rd-place teams |
| Round of 16 teams | 16 |

### Group ranking tiebreakers

| Priority | Criterion |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Match wins |
| 2 | Game differential |
| 3 | Games won |
| 4 | Head-to-head result among tied teams |
| 5 | Week 33 rating |
| 6 | Seed/Pot order |

### Third-place ranking tiebreakers

The six 3rd-place teams are ranked by the same criteria: match wins, game differential, games won, Week 33 rating, and seed order. The top four advance to the Round of 16.

---

## 5. Knockout Stage

From the Round of 16 onward, WE uses a **Bo5 single-elimination bracket**.

| Stage | Teams | Matches | Format |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Round of 16 | 16 | 8 | Bo5 |
| Quarterfinals | 8 | 4 | Bo5 |
| Semifinals | 4 | 2 | Bo5 |
| Grand Final | 2 | 1 | Bo5 |
| **Total** | - | **15** | - |

Round of 16 matches may run **two Bo5 matches per day**. From Quarterfinals onward, only **one Bo5 match per day** is scheduled.

---

## 6. Round of 16 Bracket Rules

### Fixed direct slots

| Slot | Match |
|---|---|
| R16-1 | Group A winner vs allocated 3rd-place team |
| R16-2 | Group B winner vs allocated 3rd-place team |
| R16-3 | Group C winner vs allocated 3rd-place team |
| R16-4 | Group D winner vs allocated 3rd-place team |
| R16-5 | Group E winner vs Group F runner-up |
| R16-6 | Group F winner vs Group E runner-up |
| R16-7 | Group A runner-up vs Group B runner-up |
| R16-8 | Group C runner-up vs Group D runner-up |

The four best 3rd-place teams are allocated dynamically according to the following matrix. The matrix prevents a group winner from playing a 3rd-place team from the same group in the Round of 16.

### Third-place allocation matrix

| Qualified 3rd-place groups | A1 opponent | B1 opponent | C1 opponent | D1 opponent |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ABCD | C3 | D3 | A3 | B3 |
| ABCE | C3 | E3 | A3 | B3 |
| ABCF | C3 | F3 | A3 | B3 |
| ABDE | D3 | E3 | A3 | B3 |
| ABDF | D3 | F3 | A3 | B3 |
| ABEF | E3 | F3 | A3 | B3 |
| ACDE | C3 | D3 | E3 | A3 |
| ACDF | C3 | D3 | F3 | A3 |
| ACEF | C3 | E3 | F3 | A3 |
| ADEF | D3 | E3 | F3 | A3 |
| BCDE | C3 | D3 | E3 | B3 |
| BCDF | C3 | D3 | F3 | B3 |
| BCEF | C3 | E3 | F3 | B3 |
| BDEF | D3 | E3 | F3 | B3 |
| CDEF | C3 | D3 | E3 | F3 |

### Knockout progression

| Match | Feeds into |
|---|---|
| R16-1 winner vs R16-2 winner | Quarterfinal 1 |
| R16-3 winner vs R16-4 winner | Quarterfinal 2 |
| R16-5 winner vs R16-6 winner | Quarterfinal 3 |
| R16-7 winner vs R16-8 winner | Quarterfinal 4 |
| QF1 winner vs QF2 winner | Semifinal 1 |
| QF3 winner vs QF4 winner | Semifinal 2 |
| SF1 winner vs SF2 winner | Grand Final |

## 9. Final Operational Recommendation

WE should use the full **Week 45 to Week 48** window:

- **W45 Monday to W46 Tuesday:** Group Stage
- **W46 Wednesday:** Round of 16 bracket confirmation and media day
- **W46 Thursday to Sunday:** Round of 16
- **W47 Thursday to Sunday:** Quarterfinals
- **W48 Tuesday and Thursday:** Semifinals
- **W48 Sunday:** Grand Final

This keeps the group stage compact while allowing every Quarterfinal, Semifinal, and the Grand Final to stand alone as a main event.
