# Regional Cup Tournaments PRD: COPA, APEX, EGT

**Document version:** v1.0  
**Scope:** Regional cup tournaments held during the global cup windows  
**Tournaments:** COPA, APEX, EGT  
**Primary purpose:** Regional championship cup competitions and WT qualification path for each regional champion  
**Cup windows:** Week 5, Week 9, Week 11, Week 22, Week 24, Week 28, Week 30  
**Cup match days:** Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday only  
**Regional league restriction:** No regional league matches may be held on Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday of these cup weeks.

---

## 1. Common Rules

### 1.1 Cup Windows

| Cup Week | Available Cup Days | Main Cup Stage |
|---:|---|---|
| Week 5 | Mon / Tue / Wed | Early qualifiers / Round 1 |
| Week 9 | Mon / Tue / Wed | Round 2 or Round of 64 |
| Week 11 | Mon / Tue / Wed | Round of 32 |
| Week 22 | Mon / Tue / Wed | Round of 16 |
| Week 24 | Mon / Tue / Wed | Quarterfinals |
| Week 28 | Mon / Tue / Wed | Semifinals |
| Week 30 | Mon / Tue / Wed | Finals |

### 1.2 Shared Tournament Principles

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Qualification basis | All entry positions are based on previous-year league results. |
| Venue model | Multiple venues may be used, so matches can be played simultaneously. |
| Draw type | Unless otherwise stated, draws are random. |
| Redraws | For specified later rounds, a new random draw is conducted after each round. |
| Early match format | Bo3 unless stated otherwise. |
| Quarterfinals onward | Bo5 for all three tournaments. |
| WT relevance | The COPA, APEX, and EGT champions each receive a special WT qualification slot, subject to duplicate-slot transfer rules defined in the WT PRD. |

### 1.3 Assumption Notes

COPA Round 2 contains a wording ambiguity: “L_SA 3rd to 8th, 4 teams” and “L_BR 3rd to 8th, 4 teams.” Since L_SA and L_BR are 10-team leagues and Round 1 already uses the bottom 4 teams from each league, this PRD interprets those Round 2 entries as **3rd to 6th place, 4 teams per league**.

# 7. Final Notes

- These tournaments are designed as recurring regional cup events spread across the official Monday-to-Wednesday cup windows.
- Because regional leagues resume from Thursday onward in the same weeks, all cup operations must finish within the Monday-to-Wednesday windows unless explicitly granted an exception.
- Heavy weeks such as W9 require multiple venues, simultaneous broadcast feeds, and regional production teams.
- The finals are staggered in W30 to avoid all three regional championship matches competing for the same prime broadcast slot.
